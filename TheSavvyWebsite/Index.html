The Savvy App


A free educational app created for tablets and smart phones.
The app contains video tutorials, written content and assessments, which identify where students have "gaps" in their education, and compiles a recommended syllabus specific to each user.

900+ Videos, 900+ Examples,
80+ Assessments


The Savvy App contains an abundance of offline learning material, tailored to the South African CAPS syllabus. Covering all integral concepts in both Mathematics and Physical Science from grades: 10 - 12, including prerequisites incase a fundamental concept was forgotten.

Unique features



  Never lose your progress by syncing your account.


  Our practice assessments will guide you to the answer.


  Rise to the top of our leaderboards by gaining knowledge.


  You don't need wifi access to watch, learn and assess.


  Learn from a syllabus tailored specificly to your needs.
